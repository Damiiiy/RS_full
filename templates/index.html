Have a question about the Bootcamp? Check out the FAQ

Our bootcamp is designed to give you hands-on experience through weekly tasks, stand-up meetings, and collaborative team activities. Check out the FAQs below for more details!

How long does the Bootcamp last?

The bootcamp lasts for 3 weeks, with each week focusing on a different task: individual tasks, team projects, and the final pitch.

What is the daily schedule like?

Each day starts with a stand-up meeting at 10 AM, where the team discusses progress and outlines goals for the day. You'll also work on individual tasks and collaborate on team projects.

What type of tasks will I work on during the Bootcamp?

Each week, you'll complete a different type of task: an individual task, a team project, and a final pitch that you’ll present to a panel at the end of the bootcamp.

Is the Bootcamp free of charge?

Yes, the bootcamp is completely free! We want to make it accessible for everyone interested in gaining valuable skills and experience.

Do I need any prior experience to join the Bootcamp?

No prior experience is required. Our bootcamp is designed to accommodate beginners, and we will guide you step by step through each task and project.
